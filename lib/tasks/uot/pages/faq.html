What is a SME?

A subject-matter expert (SME) or domain expert is a person who is an authority in a particular area or topic.

Who can be a SME on the Upside of Talent Marketplace?

A subject-matter expert whom typically has over 20 years of experience in Management and/or Information Technology in the domains of Government, Healthcare and Life Sciences, or Technology.

How do I sign up?

Signing up and creating a UoT SME profile is totally free. You can sign up from the homepage at www.upsideoftalent.com using your Linkedin credentials. You must have an active LinkedIn profile reflecting your experience and reputation. Once your profile is vetted, we grant access to the marketplace. After signing up, be sure to finish completing your profile to help us understand your expertise so we can help promote you in the marketplace.

What if I do not have a LinkedIn profile?

You need to establish a LinkedIn profile to be a member of the UoT marketplace. You can create a profile at www.linkedin.com.

How to refer another SMEs to the marketplace?

It’s simple! Send them a link to the UoT Marketplace and ask that they mention you under ‘referred by’ when they sign up. (Make sure to add this as a field on the signup page.)

What kind of projects and businesses are in the marketplace?

UoT has broad base of clients, from small businesses to midsize companies to non-profits. Project listings cover various durations, project types, and requirements.

How does the client select the SME?

The client has a need when they start to browse the listing of Subject Matter Experts in the UoT Marketplace. They can filter based on the SME’s area of expertise, industry, prior experience, location and other fields that you will fill out about you. Once they find candidate SMEs that seem interesting, they will “click” to start a VOIP conversation or UoT email with you. Once they are comfortable with the dialog, they can invite you to respond to a project they will post. Clients review the responses and pick a SME that is the best match. has an algorithm that works to match consultants with projects based on their profiles.

Where is the work performed?

Work location is determined by the SME. If the SME has indicated on their profile, they are willing to travel, the Client may request On Site services. Otherwise, work is done remotely and communication is through the marketplace.

How do I cancel my account?

We'll miss you if you go! If you do choose to close your account, you can do this by sending us an email to support@upsideoftalent.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can be a Client on the Upside of Talent Marketplace?

A client is an individual, small business, medium size business or Non-Profit that has a business requirement that a SME can help fulfil.

How do I sign up?

Signing up and creating a UoT Client profile is totally free. You can sign up from the homepage at www.upsideoftalent.com using your Linkedin credentials. You must have an active LinkedIn profile to participate in the UoT marketplace.

What if I do not have a LinkedIn profile?

You need to establish a LinkedIn profile to be a member of the UoT marketplace. You can create a profile at www.linkedin.com.

How do I search for the SME that will match my needs?

Get started from the Upside of Talent Homepage by clicking the Search for Experts and entering the Keywords describing the expertise you require. You can also filter on Industry, Area of Expertise, Language, Workplace and Travel.

How do I post a project?

Once you create an account and your application is approved, log in and go to Post a Project under the xx section and you can invite your selected SMEs to the project or you can open so non-identified SMEs can browse posting and ask to respond.

What does a posting include?

We provide some guidance as to the details you should include in your posting including a Project Overview, Period of Performance, Description of the Work to be Performed.

Can I message a SME directly?

You can send a direct message to a host by clicking the "Contact SME" button, located in the host profile card on the right side of the listing page.
