Introducing Upside of Talent

Imagine a global marketplace where Subject Matter Expertise is the most valuable commodity. And it’s always available on-demand.

Welcome to Upside of Talent.

UoT gives businesses instant access to the world’s best Subject Matter Experts or SMEs. And it gives experienced SME’s a powerful community to showcase and market their expertise, whether for hire or pro bono.

I launched UoT to empower senior professionals and businesses alike with a more efficient, on-demand consulting model for the digital age.

If you’re a seasoned expert, UoT gives you the support and place to still follow your heart and professional passion. Whether you were pushed into early retirement, are eager to give back or simply seeking better work-life balance, UoT gives you the flexibility to work when and how you want. On the projects you want.

And if you’re a business needing a higher level of expertise, UoT gives you a deeper bench with the experienced professionals you need — exactly when you need them.

The perfect expertise at the perfect time. That’s the power and purpose of Upside of Talent.

I invite you to grow with us and achieve your goals with us. Join the SME on-demand Community and Marketplace of Upside of Talent.

Jeanne O’Kelley, CEO
Upside of Talent
